Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental. IngrediList is built to keep your data yours.

Last Updated: October 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

IngrediList is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your recipes, shopping habits, and culinary preferences are personal and should remain private. This policy explains how IngrediList handles your information.

In short: We process everything on your device. We don't collect personal data or share it with third parties.

1. Data Collection

On-Device Only

IngrediList processes all recipe data entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • ❌ Scanned recipes or text
  • ❌ Shopping lists or ingredients
  • ❌ Photos or images you scan
  • ❌ Your name or contact details
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Browsing history

App Functionality Data (Stored Locally or in Your iCloud)

The following data is stored on your device or in your personal iCloud account:

  • ✓ App preferences (measurement system, settings toggles)
  • ✓ Selected default reminder list preference
  • ✓ Store cupboard items
  • ✓ Recipe scan history (stored locally only)
  • ✓ Subscription status (managed by Apple StoreKit)

This data:

  • Remains on your device or in your iCloud account
  • Is never transmitted to our servers (we have no servers)
  • We have no access to it
  • You control it entirely

Advertising Data (v1.0: Not Collected)

Current Status: All users enjoy an ad-free experience in v1.0. No advertising data is collected.

Future Versions: If we introduce optional ads:

  • We may collect device advertising identifiers (with your permission via ATT)
  • Google AdMob may collect ad interaction data
  • This will be clearly disclosed before implementation
  • You can always choose an ad-free subscription

2. On-Device Processing

All artificial intelligence and optical character recognition (OCR) happens entirely on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's on-device Vision framework.

This means:

  • ✓ Your recipes never leave your device
  • ✓ No internet connection required for scanning
  • ✓ We have no servers storing your data
  • ✓ Even we, the developers, cannot see what you scan
  • ✓ Processing is private, fast, and secure

3. Apple Services Integration

Apple Reminders

IngrediList integrates with Apple Reminders to create shopping lists. When you add items to Reminders:

  • Shopping lists are stored in your personal iCloud account
  • We do not have access to your Reminders data
  • Apple's privacy policies apply
  • You can revoke access anytime in: iOS Settings → IngrediList → Reminders

iCloud Key-Value Storage

IngrediList uses Apple's iCloud Key-Value Storage to sync app preferences across your devices, including:

  • Your selected default reminder list
  • Store cupboard items
  • App settings and toggles

This data:

  • Is stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Is never accessible to us
  • Syncs only between your own devices
  • Can be disabled in: iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → IngrediList

Learn more: Apple Privacy Policy

4. Third-Party Services

Advertising (Future Feature)

IngrediList includes infrastructure for advertising through Google AdMob.

Current Status (v1.0): All users enjoy an ad-free experience. No advertising data is collected.

When ads are enabled (future versions):

  • Google AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers
  • Ad interaction and performance data may be collected
  • We will request permission via App Tracking Transparency (ATT)
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in iOS Settings
  • Ad-free subscriptions are available

Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics

IngrediList does not use third-party analytics services. We do not integrate with:

  • ❌ Google Analytics
  • ❌ Facebook Analytics
  • ❌ Crash reporting services that transmit data externally
  • ❌ Behavioral tracking or profiling services

We may add privacy-respecting, on-device analytics in future versions to improve the app. We will update this policy accordingly.

In-App Purchases

If you purchase a subscription, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple via the App Store:

  • We do not process or store payment information
  • Apple manages all billing and subscriptions
  • Apple's privacy policies apply

Learn more: Apple Privacy Policy

5. Permissions

IngrediList requests the following iOS permissions:

Camera

Purpose: To scan recipes from physical sources
When: First time you tap the camera button
Required: No - you can manually type ingredients instead

Photo Library

Purpose: To scan recipes from saved images
When: First time you choose "Photo Library"
Required: No - camera-only mode available

Reminders

Purpose: To add shopping lists to Apple Reminders
When: First time you tap "Add to Reminders"
Required: No - you can view lists in-app only

All permissions are optional. If denied, IngrediList will prompt you when the feature is needed. You can manage permissions in: iOS Settings → IngrediList

6. Data Retention

Because IngrediList does not collect or store data on external servers, there is nothing for us to retain.

All data exists solely:

  • On your device (app preferences, recipe history)
  • In your personal iCloud account (synced preferences, if enabled)

If you delete IngrediList:

  • All local app data is removed from your device
  • Your Reminders lists remain in your iCloud account (managed by Apple)
  • Your iCloud preferences are deleted after 30 days (Apple policy)

7. Your Rights (GDPR & Privacy Laws)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and similar privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

Since IngrediList does not collect personal data on external servers, these rights are automatically fulfilled—there is no data for us to access, rectify, or delete. You have complete control over all data on your device and iCloud.

8. Children's Privacy

IngrediList does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

Because we collect no personal data at all, IngrediList is safe for users of all ages and meets all children's privacy requirements under COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar regulations.

Age Rating: 4+ (suitable for all ages)

9. International Users

IngrediList is available worldwide. Because all processing happens on your device:

  • ✓ No data is transferred across borders
  • ✓ No data residency concerns
  • ✓ Compliant with data localization laws
  • ✓ No "data controllers" or "processors" beyond Apple

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, and California benefit from enhanced privacy protections under local laws. IngrediList's on-device architecture inherently complies with these regulations.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect:

  • Changes in app features
  • Legal requirements
  • Industry best practices

If we make significant changes, we will notify you via:

  • In-app message or banner
  • App Store update notes
  • Email (if you've contacted us)

We will never reduce your privacy protections without notice.

11. Security

IngrediList uses industry-standard security practices:

  • All on-device processing (most secure)
  • Apple's secure frameworks (Vision, StoreKit, CloudKit)
  • No external data transmission
  • No third-party trackers or SDKs (v1.0)

Your data is as secure as your iPhone—which is to say, very secure.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or IngrediList's privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: help@ingrediList.app
Website: ingrediList.app/support

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 48 hours.

Summary

IngrediList is a privacy-first app:

  • ✅ All processing on your device
  • ✅ No data collection or transmission
  • ✅ No third-party trackers (v1.0)
  • ✅ Apple's privacy protections apply
  • ✅ You control all your data
  • ✅ Compliant with GDPR, CCPA, COPPA

Your recipes are yours. Always.
