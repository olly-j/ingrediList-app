Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental. IngrediList is built to keep your data yours.

Our Commitment to Privacy

IngrediList is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your recipes, shopping habits, and culinary preferences are personal and should remain private. This policy explains how IngrediList handles your information.

In short: We don't collect, store, or share your data. Everything happens on your device.

1. Data Collection

We collect no personal data.

IngrediList does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Scanned recipes or text
  • Shopping lists or ingredients
  • Photos or images you scan
  • Your name, email address, or contact details
  • Device identifiers or usage analytics
  • Location data

2. On-Device Processing

All AI processing and optical character recognition (OCR) happens entirely on your iPhone using Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework.

This means:

  • ✓ Your recipes never leave your device
  • ✓ No internet connection is required for scanning
  • ✓ We have no servers storing your data
  • ✓ Even we, the developers, cannot see what you scan

3. Apple Reminders Integration

IngrediList integrates with Apple Reminders to sync your shopping lists. When you add items to Reminders:

  • Data is stored in your personal iCloud account
  • We do not have access to your Reminders data
  • Apple's privacy policies apply to data in Reminders
  • You can revoke IngrediList's access at any time via iOS Settings

For more information, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

We use no third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

IngrediList does not integrate with:

  • Google Analytics or similar services
  • Facebook SDKs or social media trackers
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services that transmit data externally

If you purchase IngrediList Pro, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not process or store payment information.

5. Permissions

IngrediList requests the following iOS permissions:

  • Camera – To scan recipes from physical sources
  • Photo Library – To scan recipes from saved images
  • Reminders – To add shopping lists to Apple Reminders

All permissions are optional. If denied, IngrediList will prompt you when the feature is needed. You can manage permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

6. Data Retention

Because IngrediList does not collect or store data on external servers, there is nothing to retain. All data exists solely on your device and within your personal iCloud account (if you choose to sync via Reminders).

If you delete IngrediList from your iPhone, all local app data is removed. Your Reminders lists remain in your iCloud account and can be managed independently.

7. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and similar privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability

Since IngrediList does not collect personal data, these rights are automatically fulfilled—there is no data for us to access, rectify, or delete on external servers.

8. Children's Privacy

IngrediList does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Because we collect no personal data at all, IngrediList is safe for users of all ages.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. If we make significant changes, we will notify you via an in-app message or App Store update notes.

Last updated: October 2025
